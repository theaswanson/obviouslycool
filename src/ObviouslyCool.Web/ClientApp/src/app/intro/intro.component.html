Intro

Some say I'm the greatest streamer. Others call me a gaming legend. Still others say I play too much TF2. And they're all exactly right.

I'm crisp, the real and true gamer. I'm here to vibe and have a good time with my favorite gamers. Join me as I play through a variety of classic and modern games.

History

I was first introduced to gaming with the legendary NES, followed shortly thereafter with the N64 and GameBoy Advance. I remember playing a ton of Super Mario 64, Ocarina of Time, and the original Smash Bros., as well as Super Mario Advance 4 and Superstar Saga when I had at least two minutes to spare in the car.

My favorite console was hands-down the GameCube. I spent most of my time on this console, thus it has some of my favorite titles: Paper Mario: The Thousand-Year Door, Wind Waker, and Double Dash, just to name a few. The day my brother and I got Melee is still a distinct memory in my mind.

Of course, consoles weren't my only exposure to gaming. Flash games were a big pastime of mine and I spent hours playing them, both at home on our wicked-fast dial-up connection as well as at school. The massively popular games Raze and Strike Force Heroes by Sky9 Games were some of my favorite titles.

While on the Sky9 community forums, I was introduced to Team Fortress 2 which brought me into the world of true PC gaming. This was the first game I ever played on Steam, and shortly thereafter I got my hands on Portal and Half-Life 2. I also remember picking up Minecraft at a Walmart and spending my first night in my pristine dirt hut. One thing led to another, and now I've played quite a number of PC games.

Like most gamers, I have a huge backlog of games to finish. There's even games from my childhood that I have never seen to the end. I plan to change that.
